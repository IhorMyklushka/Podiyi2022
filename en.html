About the project "Events 2022 through the eyes of Ukrainian artists. Collection of artworks"

On February 24, 2022, a full-scale Russian invasion of Ukraine took place, which was accompanied by the active spread of Russian propaganda. Contrary to the constant flow of disinformation and despite the difficult situation in which the whole Ukrainian people found themselves, creators from all over Ukraine — illustrators and artists, polygraphists and poets were not silent — since then they have been reproducing and helping to spread the true state of affairs in their media spaces.

The collection "Events 2022 through the eyes of Ukrainian artists" includes about a hundred different works by talented artists. Some of the works have already been spreaded widely on the Internet, some could be seen on the street — on the walls, bus stops, on the structures for outdoor advertising (over road banners, billboards, cars, etc.), some could be found in the hands of Protestants who came to the rally, some are known only in a small audience. But all of them are connected by the fact that they lift the patriotic spirit of the nation, bringing faith and hope in our hearts, unite the entire Ukrainian people and unite with people from all over the world who are not indifferent to the situation in Ukraine.

Publication features

The project is implemented within the coursework and is not commercial. The search for works for the electronic collection took place on the social network Instagram, so before each series of artworks there will be a nickname with a link to the profile so that you can easily find the authors. Also, for convenience, for each work of the artist, in the lower right corner, there is a link to this artwork on Instagram.

Hello!
